This example introduces D3's animation technique called transitioning. Use the radio buttons at the bottom to transition between the Feb7 and Feb8 datasets in an animated way. The animation enhances the visualization by allowing the eye to follow the changes in the leaderboard.

This is possibly the most difficult example in the course. It teaches about the full enter-update-exit pipeline that is an innovation of D3. The course will describe a pattern to use to manage the complexity of this pipeline.
