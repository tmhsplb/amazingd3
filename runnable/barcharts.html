This example shows the same dataset presented in two different styles of barchart. The barchart on the left is sometimes called a rowchart. It is often useful to use HTML controls to experiment with different parameterizations of a D3 chart. The charts below are parameterized by bar height and bar width, respectively. In each case, a slider input control has been provided to experiment with this parametrization.
