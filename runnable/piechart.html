Technically the chart below is a donut chart since it has a hole in the middle. The hole is created by setting the innerRadius of the pie to a positive value. When the innerRadius of the pie is set to 0 there will be no hole and the chart will be a true pie chart. There is also an outerRadius. The outerRadius determines the radius of the chart measured from its center.
