This example is Example 94 in a screencast of a D3 talk given by Curran Kelleher to the Bay Area D3 Meetup Group in April 2015. The display is a scatterplot of 4,272 points representing cities with a population of more than 100,000. The latitude and longitude of a city are linearly mapped as circles to a 440px x 130px SVG container. The radius of the circle representing a city is set proportional to its population measured in millions.
